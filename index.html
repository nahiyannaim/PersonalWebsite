What keeps me busy

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Manitoba, currently working as a Software Developer. As an extremely motivated individual with a strong desire to learn new things every day I have a deep passion for technology and full stack software development with interests in not only learning about latest developments in the industry but also applying them practically to make things better, faster and easier.

Outside of Computer Science, I am extremely passionate about music, concerts, travelling and photography. Most of my playlists consists of Indie, Alternative Rock and Pop songs but I am always open to anything that's a treat to the ears. On weekends, I'm either going out or practicing with my acoustic guitar and plus if there's any good concert in town, there's a good chance you'll find me there. Moreover, being a fan of waterfalls, mountains and everything nature, I frequently take road trips with my friends to check off places from my bucket list, like major national parks and lakes including Banff, Jasper, Yoho and Waterton. My favourites so far include Moraine Lake, Emerald Lake and Bears Hump Waterton. Additionally, I love taking nature landscapes and portraits with DSLR cameras equipped with telephoto and macro lenses.

I believe a good balance between work and personal life is extremely important because they both complement each other in shaping a successful individual. I am always looking for ways to improve myself, both personally and professionally. Most importantly, I look for enthusiasm in any and every work I do to stay motivated and keep things interesting at all times. I love meeting new people and learning new things from them so feel free to get in touch with me any time!
