About me:

Hello World! I am currently a 3rd year Computer Science Major (Co-op) student at the University of Manitoba.
As an extremely motivated individual with a strong desire to learn new things every day I have a deep passion for technology and
software development with interests in not only learning about latest developments in the industry but also applying them
practically to make things better, faster and easier.
Skill set:

  • • Course-level experience in: Java, C, C++, Ruby, LC-3 Assembly and SQL Databases.
  • • Familiar with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery web development.
  • • Knowledge of Object-orientated programming, Agile development, TDD, SOLID design principles, software design patterns.

Tools & Technologies:
  • • Git/Github, Eclipse, Visual Studio Code, Android Studio, OpenAPI 3, Swagger editor, Slack, Microsoft Office, Adobe Photoshop.
  • • Experience using Windows, MacOS, Android, and iOS systems.

    • I believe a good balance between work and personal life is extremely important because they both complement each other
    in shaping a successful individual. I am always looking for ways to improve myself, both personally and professionally.
    Most importantly, I look for enthusiasm in any and every work I do to stay motivated and keep things interesting at all times.
