What keeps me busy

I am currently a 3rd year Computer Science Major (Co-op) student at the University of Manitoba. As an extremely motivated individual with a strong desire to learn new things every day I have a deep passion for technology and software development with interests in not only learning about latest developments in the industry but also applying them practically to make things better, faster and easier.

Outside of Computer Science, I am extremely passionate about music and love making covers of Indie, Alternative Rock and Pop songs using my acoustic guitar. I am also a fan of waterfalls and mountains and in May 2017, I went on a road trip with my friends to Western Canada covering the major national parks and lakes including Banff, Jasper and Waterton. Additionally, I love taking nature landscapes and portraits with DSLR cameras with telephoto and macro lenses.

I believe a good balance between work and personal life is extremely important because they both complement each other in shaping a successful individual. I am always looking for ways to improve myself, both personally and professionally. Most importantly, I look for enthusiasm in any and every work I do to stay motivated and keep things interesting at all times. I love meeting new people and learning new things from them so feel free to get in touch with me any time!
